CreditProfile

CreditProfile.json

Entity > CreditProfile

Credit profile for the party (containing credit scoring, ...). By default only the current credit profile is retrieved. It can be used as a list to give the party credit profiles history, the first one in the list will be the current one.


Properties of CreditProfile

PropertyTypeDescription
creditProfileDatestringThe date the profile was established
creditRiskRatingintegerThis is an integer whose value is used to rate the risk
creditScoreintegerA measure of a person or organizations creditworthiness calculated on the basis of a combination of factors such as their income and credit history
validForTimePeriodThe period for which the profile is valid
