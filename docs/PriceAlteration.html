PriceAlteration

PriceAlteration.json

Entity > PriceAlteration

Is an amount, usually of money, that modifies the price charged for an order item.


Properties of PriceAlteration

PropertyTypeDescription
applicationDurationintegerDuration during which the alteration applies on the order item price (for instance 2 months free of charge for the recurring charge)
descriptionstringA narrative that explains in detail the semantics of this order item price alteration
namestringA short descriptive name such as "Monthly discount"
priceTypestringA category that describes the price such as recurring, one time and usage.
priorityintegerPriority level for applying this alteration among all the defined alterations on the order item price
recurringChargePeriodstringCould be month, week...
unitOfMeasurestringCould be minutes, GB...
pricePriceundefined
